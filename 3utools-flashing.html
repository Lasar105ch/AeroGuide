Flashing your iPhone, iPad, or iPod can help you fix issues, downgrade iOS, or reinstall the system fresh. 3uTools is a powerful, free Windows tool that simplifies flashing and jailbreaking for iOS devices.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Download and Install 3uTools

Go to the official site: https://www.3u.com/
Download the latest version of 3uTools.
Install it on your Windows PC and launch the program.

2. Connect Your iPhone/iPad/iPod

Use your USB Lightning cable to connect your device to the PC.
Trust the computer if prompted on the device.
3uTools will detect your device and show details like model, iOS version, and battery status.

3. Backup Your Device (Highly Recommended)

In 3uTools, go to the Toolbox tab.

Choose your backup location and start the backup process.

4. Download the Correct iOS Firmware

Click the Flash & JB tab.
3uTools will automatically suggest the latest compatible firmware for your device.
You can also select other versions if you want to downgrade or upgrade.
Click Download next to the firmware you want.

5. Start Flashing

6. Wait for Flashing to Complete

The process will take several minutes.
Do NOT disconnect your device during flashing.
